Elon Musk

Elon Musk is an South African-born American enterpreneur and businessman who founded X.com in 1999 (which later became PayPal), SpaceX in 2002 and Tesla motors in 2003. Musk became a multimillionaire in his late 20s when he had sold his start-up company Zip2, to a division of Compact Computers. He as an estimated net worth of around 59.2 billion dollars and is listed as the 16th richest person in the world according to Forbes. His ultimate goal is to make life multiplantery and help humanity explore beyond earth.
